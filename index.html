🍱 7 Days Meal Plan

Objective: realistic Malaysian-style meal plan to stay lean, support training, and stick close to ~1900 kcal.

🧠 How To Use This Plan

Portion control is very important:
Rice: 1 cup max per meal
Oil: keep low
Protein: do not skip

⚠️ Rules To Stay Lean

  • No sugary drinks such as teh tarik or soft drinks
  • Limit fried food
  • Protein every meal
  • Stick to around 1900 kcal

🔥 Supplement Use

  • Whey: post-workout
  • USN Fast Grow Anabolic: avoid during cut
  • Pre-workout drink: OK

💬 Real Talk

This plan works because it is realistic, flexible, and sustainable. It uses Malaysian-style food, keeps protein high, and avoids making the diet too boring.

⚙️ Auto Weight Suggestion Rules

Green light: If all working sets reach the top of the rep range, next session suggests adding weight.

Repeat: If reps are inside the target range, repeat the same weight and aim for more reps.

Reduce or retry: If any set misses the minimum rep target, repeat or slightly reduce the weight.

Barbell/machine: +2.5kg DB/isolation: +1kg Leg press: +5kg

🗓️ 16-Week Structure

Weeks 1–4: Cut. Maintain strength and keep form strict.
Week 5: Transition/deload. Reduce volume around 20%.
Weeks 6–16: Lean bulk. Push progressive overload.

☁️ iCloud Backup Guide

Export (Save your data):
1. Tap 📤 Export Data
2. Choose Save to Files
3. Select iCloud Drive and create a folder like GymTracker

Import (Restore your data):
1. Tap 📥 Import Data
2. Select your saved file from iCloud
3. Your data will load automatically

Tip: Backup once per week after your last workout.
